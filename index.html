How can I place an order online?

1. Write to WhatsApp or direct: our specialists will send you photos of glasses (maximum 10) suitable for you on WhatsApp;
2. You choose the glasses you like (optical / sunglasses), if you need to install lenses, we will make them this or the next day;
3. We place an order, and you pay online payment.

Is it possible to deliver glasses if I live in another city?

Delivery across Almaty is carried out on the same day or the next day.

How long is the delivery time and how?

Delivery in RK usually takes 2-4 days. You will be given a track number with which you can track the parcel.

Is there a refund?

Unfortunately no.

Are there any guarantees for the glasses made?

Yes, of course, we give a guarantee for hidden factory defects, and we always go to meet the client.

Will the glasses break upon delivery?

We send glasses in a hard case, there were no breakages even once. We also indicate that the cargo is fragile.
